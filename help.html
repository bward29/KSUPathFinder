Getting Started

The AI Academic Advisor is designed to help you find information about courses, majors, registration, deadlines, financial aid, and campus resources. Simply type your question in the chat box and press Enter or click the send button.

Quick Tip

Be specific with your questions to get the most accurate information. The more details you provide, the better the advisor can help you.

Example Questions

Here are some examples of questions you can ask the AI Academic Advisor:

What you tell me about the Computer Science major?
When does fall registration begin?
What is the tuition rate for in-state students?
Can you provide information about CS35101?
What academic resources are available on campus?
What is the GPA requirement for good academic standing?

Topic Categories

The AI Academic Advisor can provide information on the following topics:

Category Examples
Courses Course details, prerequisites, credit hours
Majors Degree programs, requirements, career opportunities
Registration Registration dates, process, course load
Academic Calendar Semester dates, breaks, final exam periods
Financial Information Tuition rates, payment deadlines, financial aid
Campus Resources Academic Success Center, Writing Center, Library
Academic Policies Standing, grading, honors, enrollment requirements

Troubleshooting

If you're not getting the information you need:

  • Try rephrasing your question with more specific details
  • Check if your question falls into one of the topic categories listed above
  • For complex questions, break them down into simpler questions
  • If you need human assistance, please contact Benjamin Ward at bward29@kent.edu

Remember

The AI Academic Advisor provides general information and guidance. For personalized academic planning, please schedule an appointment with your academic advisor.
